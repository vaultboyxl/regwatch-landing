What an alert looks like

What changed: Under HB 2789 (HHSC letter PGL-26-11001), every licensed center must now carry liability insurance — or notify every parent in writing that it doesn't.
What you must do: 1) Confirm your liability policy is active and meets the requirement. 2) If you're not covered, send the required written notice to all parents and keep proof on file.
By when: Effective January 13, 2026 — already in force.
Risk if ignored: A cited deficiency on your public record — visible to every parent who searches your center.
